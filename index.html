Destinations

There is no shortage of places to travel in Brazil! It has a wonderful diversity, stunning landscapes and cities that offer the most varied qualities. Some destinations stand out for their good tourism infrastructure and others for their large number of attractions, cultural inheritance or abundance of natural beauty.

National Parks

Brazil is full of beautiful natural landscapes and many of it is protected through the creation of National Parks. These parks constitute protected areas so that nature remains in its best condition. Brazil currently has 74 national parks, managed by the Chico Mendes Institute for Biodiversity Conservation (ICMBio).

How to send money to Brazil

Sending money to Brazil is more complex than most foreign citizens might expect. Brazil has a history of extreme regulation of money entering and leaving the country. Commercial banks in Brazil such as Bradesco and Itaú may take weeks to release funds until clients have provided many documents required and their slow currency exchange departments have thoroughly reviewed these documents.
